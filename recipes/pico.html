Pico de gallo

I love fresh vegetables, and one of my favorite uses for them is in pico de gallo. It's such a wonderful topping or side for so many dishes and adds some vibrant colors too!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs ripe tomatoes, cut into 1/4 - 1/2 inch pieces.
  • Salt
  • 1/2 large white onion, finely diced.
  • 1 or 2 serrano or jalapeno chilles, finely diced with seeds and membranes removed (if you want less spice)
  • 1/2 cup finely diced cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon freshly-squeezed lime juice

Steps

  1. Season tomatoes with salt and toss to combine. Strain, then set aside.
  2. combine drained tomatoes with onion, chiles, cilantro and lime juice, toss to mix. Season with salt to taste.
